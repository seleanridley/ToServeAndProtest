Your SafeWatch

Here is your personal guide for your SafeWatch. Only you can access this page through your unique serial number. DO NOT share this number with anyone who isn't a TSAP member or a part of the task force.

Explore your TSAP SafeWatch

Rescue Rides

If you are in a situation that you feel could put you in danger, you can send an alert through the 'Rescue Rides' app that will alert 5 nearby task force drivers in the area to come and drive you to whatever location is most safe for them.

Check-In

Click the 'Check-In' app above to navigate! When you signed up to receive your watch, you should have been given the chance to register an accountability buddy who will be given secured access to the app. If you enter a situation that you feel may endanger you, you will be able to “check in” when you enter the space, log how much time you plan to be in the space, an option to send your accountability buddy your location and “check out” once you leave. If the allotted time passes and you have not checked out of the space, you will receive three alerts before your accountability buddy is alerted on the app.

Accessing your Saved Buddies

Through the 'Saved' app, you can access all your saved accountability buddies from the TSAP network.

Accessing your Messages

Here, on the 'Messages' app, you can access all your messages from the people in your network.
